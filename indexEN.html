Audi R8 Coupé V10 quattro

Proximity to motorsports, great power, uncompromising forms - no other car embodies the genes of Audi as eloquently as the Audi R8. It is a kind of quintessence of the brand's experience in competitive sports and in the creation of exclusive cars. Audi R8 Coupé V10 quattro is one hundred percent representative of Audi to the last detail - and at the highest level.

Chassis: precision in every respect

Along with power, in motorsport it is always of particular importance how precisely and fully it can be transported on the road. The main part of the responsibility in this direction falls on the chassis: the double triangular aluminum struts are a solution used in the suspension of each of the four wheels of the Audi R8. The Audi R8 comes standard with an Audi drive select system with four operating modes. Audi magnetic ride is an option for adaptive shock absorbers, which allows you to choose individual suspension settings, and the characteristics change for each wheel within milliseconds.

The new steering wheel adjustment responds even faster to your commands and provides extremely accurate feedback on the contact of the wheels with the road, and the dynamic steering offered at the customer's request changes its settings according to speed and provides even more precise handling. The steel discs of the brake system are perforated and with forced ventilation. Their Wave design clearly shows how seriously Audi looks at the lightweight technology in the R8. The brake calipers are available in black as standard, and are also available in red at the customer's request.

The design: the perfect line

Designed to win: with its outstretched cockpit, accentuated fenders and distinctive diffuser, the new Audi R8 showcases many racing elements in its vision and recalls Audi's success in motorsport.The low horizontal lines add extra width to the front of the Audi R8, giving it an even sportier look. The profile is dominated by the characteristic "blades", and the rear with its ventilation grille across the entire width and the new oval tips of the exhaust system is a fitting finish to an impressive design. The standard 19-inch wheels can be replaced by new 20-inch milled wheels with a special design and three coating options.

The interior: elegant and focused around the driver

The proximity to motorsport is also clear inside the Audi R8: Directly in front of you in the form of a contoured arc are all the controls - just like in a racing car. The sports seats with electric and pneumatic adjustment have Feinnappa leather upholstery. New for the Audi R8 Coupé V10 quattro are the offered color combinations of pastel gray with contrasting stitching in Field gray, Palomino brown with steel gray stitching and black with utopia blue stitching. Naturally, there are no limits to your wishes - Audi exclusive has almost inexhaustible additional color choices.

Audi Virtual Cockpit

The idea of ​​an interior that keeps the driver's concentration on the road to the maximum is implemented digitally here through the combination of MMI Navigation plus and Audi virtual cockpit. The whole ergonomic concept is created with the basic idea that you do not have to take your hands off the steering wheel and do not look away from the road. The 12.3-inch diameter Audi virtual cockpit displays a tachometer, and there are other indicators around it, such as power, g-acceleration, lap time, engine and transmission oil temperature, and pressure and temperature. on the tires. The MMI command system works with intelligent voice recognition and can be operated almost entirely without having to take your hands off the steering wheel.

Individualization: exclusive taste

The external appearance of the Audi R8 is up to you - Audi exclusive gives you the opportunity to order the color of the paintwork entirely according to your preferences. The side elements can be ordered with a choice of five colors or an individual color. With the help of a special logo or inscription, for the first time you get the opportunity to make your Audi R8 even more personal.

V10-atmospheric engine: central heart with a lot of power

The heart of the Audi R8 is its V10-atmospheric engine located in front of the rear axle. 5.2 The FSI unit further sharpens the sporty character of the car with its gigantic power - the maximum torque is 560 Nm and is reached at 6200 rpm. The close connection with the high technologies of motor sports is evident in many details. For example, the lubrication system with dry sump allows extremely low engine mounting - which further contributes to the precise handling of the Audi R8. The specific ignition order of the ten cylinders brings with it an extremely characteristic engine sound.
